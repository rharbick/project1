About Us

Formed by a group of former circus children, Cosmic Cookies has embarked on the journey of bringing sweet treats to the entire galaxy. Now with over 5 locations, Cosmic Cookies is the fastest growing cookie distributor in the solar system!

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring sweet and tasty treats to the entire universe! Our scientists have been working non stop to update our delivery systems with nuclear fusion engines. Not only can more people get treats, our research into nuclear fusion will fundementally change humanity for the better.
